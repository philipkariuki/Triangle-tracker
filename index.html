Triangle Tracker

Here is a triangle tracker made from JavaScript. It uses user input data to determine whether a triangle meets the following criteria:

  • Isosceles: If two sides are the same then the triangle is iscosceles.
  • Equilateral: If all three sides are the same then the triangle is equilateral.
  • Scalene: If none of the three side match in size then the triangle is scalene.
  • Not a triangle: Unless the sum of any two sides of the triangle is equal to or less than the third remaining side, then a triangle cannot be formed.
